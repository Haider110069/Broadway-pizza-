Our Story

From our family to yours, Broadway Pizza has been serving delicious meals since 1995.

Passion in Every Slice

Founded by Italian immigrant Marco Rossi, Broadway Pizza began as a small family-owned pizzeria with a big dream. With recipes passed down through generations, we've maintained the authentic taste of Italy while embracing modern culinary techniques.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We source the finest ingredients locally and import specialty items directly from Italy. From our hand-stretched dough to our homemade sauces, every element of our pizza is crafted with precision and care.
